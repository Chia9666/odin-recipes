Sarawak Style Fried Rice

Fried Rice from Malaysia

A plate of hot cooked white rice is always our preferred starch base in most of our meals — in some regions, locals take a meal with rice for breakfast, and others for lunch and dinner.

This Chinese fried rice recipe is a classic, lightly seasoned with savoury spices and natural ingredients that not only work as a tasty dish but look vibrant and lively as well.

Ingredients:

  • Cooked Rice
  • Carrots
  • Soy Sauce
  • Chinese Sausages
  • White Pepper
  • Cooking Oil
  • Egg
  • Garlic
  • Oyster Sauce
  • Sugar

Directions:

  1. Heat wok with cooking oil, sauté garlic with fresh prawns.
  2. Add egg, cook until mixed well.
  3. Add diced carrots, rice, Oyster sauce, sugar and white pepper. Mix well and serve hot.
